How to Play
 
 

One Rook Endgame

Force your opponent's king to the last rank/file.

Push your king to cut off the second rank/file.

Move your rook to the last rank/file for checkmate!

Two Rook Endgame

Keep your rooks away from the enemy pieces!

Advance your rooks rank by rank or file by file to force the enemy king back.

End your "leapfrog" on the last rank/file for checkmate!

Two Bishop Endgame

You will need both bishops on different colors!

Play this similarly to the Two Rook Endgame, cutting off diagonals.

Force the opponent's king to the corner closest to your king.

Use your king to cut off the diagonals your bishops do not cover. Move your bishop to the long diagonal for checkmate!
